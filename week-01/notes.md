## In My Own Words

**What is the CIA Triad, in your own words?**
Confidentiality ensures that sensitive information is accessed only by authorized individuals, systems, or processes. It is designed to prevent unauthorized disclosure of data.
Integrity ensures that data remains accurate, complete, and trustworthy. It guarantees that the information has not been altered, deleted, or tampered with by unauthorized parties—either accidentally or maliciously.
Availability ensures that authorized users have reliable and timely access to data, systems, and resources when they need them.
